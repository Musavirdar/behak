Preserving Kashmir's Textile Legacy

Our story begins in the pristine valleys of Kashmir, where the world's finest Changthangi goats produce the rarest pashmina fiber. At altitudes of over 14,000 feet, these extraordinary animals develop an undercoat so fine and soft, it has been prized by royalty for centuries.
